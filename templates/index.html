Our Story

Mitho Chiya isn't just a tea shop—it's a celebration of Nepal's rich tea heritage. Born from a passion for authentic flavors and warm hospitality.

We source our tea leaves directly from the misty hills of Ilam, ensuring every cup carries the essence of the Himalayas. Our snacks are prepared using traditional recipes passed down through generations.
